About

Who I am and how I work.

I’m a results-driven PLM Techno-functional Architect, and Business Systems Analyst with over 14 years of experience delivering PLM implementations and integrations across Medical Devices, Hi-Tech, Aerospace, Semiconductor, Industrial Equipment, Consumer Goods, and Healthcare.

My core strengths are in Siemens Teamcenter, Oracle Agile PLM, Windchill, and MechWorks PDM, combined with hands-on integration work using T4X/T4O, Oracle Fusion Middleware, APIs, and Power Platform. I design integration patterns that connect PLM with ERP (Oracle EBS, Syspro, SAP, Dynamics), ALM (Polarion), MES, and QMS to create a reliable digital thread.

I’m comfortable operating between business and IT leading workshops, clarifying requirements, running fit-gap analyses, and then working with technical teams to make sure solutions are practical, testable, and supportable. I enjoy simplifying complex landscapes and aligning tools, data, and processes so teams can focus on delivery rather than firefighting integrations.

Outside work, I enjoy travelling and following sports, which have both shaped how I think about teamwork, adaptability, and continuous improvement when working with cross-functional and global teams.
