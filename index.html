About

Who I am and how I work.

I am a PLM Techno-functional Architect and Business Systems Analyst with more than 14 years of experience delivering PLM implementations and integrations across medical devices, hi-tech, aerospace, semiconductor, industrial equipment, consumer goods, and healthcare.

My core strengths are in Siemens Teamcenter, Oracle Agile PLM, Windchill, and MechWorks PDM, combined with hands-on integration work using T4X and T4O, Oracle Fusion Middleware, APIs, and Power Platform. I design integration patterns that connect PLM with ERP (Oracle EBS, Syspro, SAP, Dynamics), ALM (Polarion), MES, and QMS to create a reliable digital thread.

I am comfortable operating between business and IT: leading workshops, clarifying requirements, running fit-gap analyses, and then working with technical teams to make sure solutions are practical, testable, and supportable. I enjoy simplifying complex landscapes and aligning tools, data, and processes so teams can focus on delivery rather than firefighting integrations.

Outside work, I enjoy travelling and following sports, which both shape how I think about teamwork, adaptability, and continuous improvement when working with cross-functional and global teams.
