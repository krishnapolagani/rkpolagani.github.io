About

A quick view of my background and how I like to work.

I am a PLM techno-functional architect and business systems analyst with more than fourteen years of experience delivering PLM implementations and integrations across medical devices, hi-tech, aerospace, semiconductor, industrial equipment, consumer goods and healthcare.

My core strengths are in Siemens Teamcenter, Oracle Agile PLM, PTC Windchill and MechWorks PDM, combined with hands-on integration work using T4X and T4O, Oracle Fusion Middleware, APIs and Power Platform. I design integration patterns that connect PLM with ERP systems such as Oracle EBS, Syspro, SAP and Microsoft Dynamics, as well as ALM, MES and QMS platforms.

I enjoy working in the space between business and IT. I run workshops, clarify requirements, perform fit gap analysis and then work with technical teams to make sure solutions are practical, testable and supportable. I like simplifying complex landscapes and aligning tools, data and processes so teams can focus on delivery instead of constantly firefighting integrations.

Outside work I enjoy travelling and following sports. Both influence how I think about teamwork, resilience and continuous improvement when I work with cross functional and global teams.
