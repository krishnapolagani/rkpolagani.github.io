Sports & Fitness

Sports have always been a big part of my life not just as entertainment, but as a source of energy, discipline, and motivation. Whether it's the strategy behind a close cricket chase, the atmosphere of a baseball game, or the precision of Formula 1 racing, I enjoy the blend of competition, teamwork, and passion that sports bring.

Interests

Sports I follow or play

Cricket – Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH)

I'm a dedicated supporter of SRH. I enjoy the strategic side of T20 cricket — from powerplay momentum to death-over execution. Watching SRH's bowling strength and comeback performances makes every match exciting.

Baseball – Toronto Blue Jays

The Blue Jays introduced me to baseball's unique rhythm and tactical depth. I enjoy following their games, especially the teamwork, pitching battles, and the atmosphere around home runs at Rogers Centre.

Formula 1 – Max Verstappen

I'm a strong admirer of Max Verstappen's racing style — aggressive, calculated, and consistently competitive. F1 combines engineering excellence and driver skill, and following Max’s races keeps the sport thrilling every season.
