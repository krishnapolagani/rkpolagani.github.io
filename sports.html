Sports & Fitness

Energy, discipline, and inspiration — on and off the field.

Sports have always been a big part of my life. They bring balance, competition, teamwork, and a sense of rhythm to my routine. From the intensity of IPL cricket to the tactical pauses in baseball and the engineering brilliance of Formula 1 — every sport brings something unique that inspires me personally and professionally.

Whether I’m watching SRH at a stadium, cheering for the Blue Jays in Toronto, or following the precision of Max Verstappen in F1 — sports keep me motivated and grounded.

Interests

Sports I Follow Closely

Cricket — Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH)

A long-standing SRH supporter. I enjoy the strategic, chess-like feel of T20 cricket — powerplays, strike rotations, death bowling, and momentum shifts that can change a match in minutes.

Baseball — Toronto Blue Jays

Moving to Canada introduced me to baseball. The atmosphere at Rogers Centre, the crowd chants, and the pitcher–batter duels make it a sport that’s both relaxing and tense at the same time.

Formula 1 — Max Verstappen

F1 combines elite engineering with fearless driving. Max Verstappen’s aggressive yet controlled style makes every race worth watching. Strategy, tyre management, and team coordination are all part of the thrill.
